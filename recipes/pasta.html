Pasta

A simple dish to add carbs to your diet.

Cook Time
  • 25 mins
Ingredients
  • Pasta 1 cups
  • Salt 2 pinch
  • Splash of Oil
  • Water to cover the pasta
Instructions
  1. Add boiling Water and Salt to medium pot with lid
  2. Set it to high and wait for it to boil
  3. Leave on boil then add the pasta
  4. Cook until the pasta is al-dented
  5. Strain the water out
  6. Add oil to prevent sticking
  7. Serve
