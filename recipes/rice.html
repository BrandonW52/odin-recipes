Rice

A simple dish to add carbs to your diet.

Cook Time

  • 25 mins

Ingredients

  • Rice 1/2 cups
  • Salt 1 pinch
  • Water 1 cups

Instructions

  1. Add Rice, Water and Salt to small pot with lid
  2. Set it to high and wait for it to boil.
  3. Leave on boil for 4 minutes
  4. Turn off the heat, leave pot on stove
  5. Wait of lid to stop steaming
  6. Serve
