Potato Salad

A simple dish to add carbs to your diet.

Cook Time
  • 30 mins
Ingredients
  • Potatoes 4
  • Mayo
  • Salt 1 pinch
  • Water enough to cover
Instructions
  1. Add Water and Salt to medium pot with lid
  2. Set it to high and wait for it to boil
  3. Peel and cube the potatoes
  4. Add the cubed potatoes
  5. Cook until soft
  6. Drain out the water
  7. Let it cool down
  8. Add Mayo to taste
  9. Serve
